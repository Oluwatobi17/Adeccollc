Personal Assistance

Position Overview:

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Personal & Business Assistant to coordinate and manage a variety of personal and professional tasks. This role requires someone detail-oriented, trustworthy, and capable of handling multiple responsibilities with efficiency. The ideal candidate will assist with scheduling, errands, and administrative duties while maintaining confidentiality and professionalism.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate and manage a complex schedule, including both personal and work-related appointments.
  • Maintain and prioritize multiple task lists to ensure deadlines are met.
  • Handle personal and business errands, including purchasing gift items such as accessories, Bitcoin, and gifts for foster care initiatives.
  • Maintain accurate and organized accounting document files.
  • Enter daily worksheets into designated systems for record-keeping and reporting.
  • Assist in managing personal and business-related correspondence.

Qualifications:

  • No experience in administrative or personal assistant roles is required.
  • Exceptional organizational and multitasking skills is an added advantage.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to handle sensitive information with discretion.
  • Familiarity with accounting and document management.
  • Basic knowledge of cryptocurrencies (e.g., Bitcoin) is a plus.
  • Reliable transportation for running errands.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Work Schedule and Compensation:

  • Part-time position: 2 days per week.
  • Compensation: $600 per week.

How to Apply:

If you are a dependable and organized individual with a passion for helping others manage their busy lives, we’d love to hear from you! Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter detailing your relevant experience.
